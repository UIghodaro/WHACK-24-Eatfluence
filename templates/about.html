About Our Vision


You hungry yet?


  1. Inspiration - Keeping it short, our vision started from a simple question, "how do people find those truly life changing experiences"?
    The simple answer was it's complicated, the longer answer was "by chance" or "via another's recommendation"...
    BUT. What if we could collate the verified good experiences into one? That is what we set out to do.

  2. So what does it do? - Our app retrieves the most popular and trendy tik toks to do with restaurants in an area of your choice,
    before simplifying these tik-toks and presenting the option to go or not to you!

  3. Why We're proud of it - It was damn hard. Don't ask us how we did it, we just did it and it was hard. (please someone get that song reference)
